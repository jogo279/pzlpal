Crossword Puzzle Digitizer and Solver

Upload a crossword puzzle picture from your computer or a URL.


Guidelines

The allowed file types are .jpg, .gif, .png, and .bmp, and the maximum image size is 10MB.

The picture should contain the crossword grid and puzzle clues.

Images should be high resolution (i.e. big text). Screenshots may or may not work.

The picture should be taken from a bird's eye view, and not from an angle.
